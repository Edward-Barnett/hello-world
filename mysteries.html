The strange story of the mysterious figure unearthed in my back yard.

What was this thing? What did it want from me? Why did I keep it in my house for so many years?

The Find

Some years ago, I was digging around in the yard when... I found something buried near the ficus tree. Not deep in the ground but it appeared to have been there for a long time. With haste that I would soon regret, I dug it out and cleaned it up.

And so I discovered the mysterious object that would puzzle me for several years.

The object is about four inches high -- a more accurate measurement is not possible because I couldn't find a ruler.

Of some hard, brittle material, I believe the original coloring has been affected by time spent in the ground. Red coloring atop the figure's "head" is well-preserved under some transparent material. The overall impression is of a man in some sort of protective suit.

I was able to satisfy myself that the object was manufactured, and not the mummified remains of a once living creature.

Presumably terrestrial, how old is it? Pre-Columbian? How could it be reliably dated?

The expression on the face is mysterious... Mocking.

Effects of the Object

My first reaction to the object was curiosity. My second reaction was fear.

My third reaction was one of surprise. Surprise that I could fear the object.

I came to realize that, every year since I found it, I became ill for a short period, usually in the winter.

Also I had to replace my car battery.

Sounds emitted by the Object.

The object emits no sound detectable to the human ear. Although my hearing is not perfect.

An Important clue.

On the objects posterior, some characters are stamped. They appear to be in English.

Finally I could take it no more.

It got to the point where I was sick of not knowing, and remembered that I still had the thing.

I had to do something.

To my surprise...

After a brief Google search I found a similar object for sale on eBay. It was some sort of toy astronaut.

The one I have is badly discolored, having been in the dirt for so long, so I don't think I can get much for it. Besides, I failed to unearth the original packaging.

A conclusion.

No child lived on these premises before we moved in. so I believe a dog stole the toy from a neighborhood kid and buried it.

I feel bad for the kid.

If the toy was yours, contact me and I'll be sure to get it to you.

A more important conclusion.

I dug up a piece of trash and kept it in my house for several years.
