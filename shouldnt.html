The strange story of my toaster

We bought our toaster at Costco a couple of years ago, and I like it.

But I read the instructions, and it states, explicitly, that using it to heat toaster pastries will void the warranty.

I didn't have to think about this for very long. I can buy another toaster, but what's the point of having a toaster if I can't use it to heat Pop-Tarts?
